Roti Recipe

Description

Roti is a soft, round Indian flatbread made from whole wheat flour. It is a staple food in many Indian homes and is simple to make with minimal ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • Water as needed
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with water to form a smooth dough and let it rest for a few minutes.
  2. Roll the dough into thin circles and cook on a hot pan until both sides puff up and turn light brown.
