Idli

Faruq Sayyed, CC BY-SA 4.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Idli is a soft, steamed South Indian breakfast made from a fermented batter of rice and lentils. It is light, healthy, and easy to digest, making it a popular everyday food.

Ingredients

a
  • 1 cup idli rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Soak the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for a few hours, then grind them into a smooth batter. Add salt and let the batter ferment overnight.
