Dosa

Roland, CC BY 2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Dosa is a thin, crispy South Indian pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It is simple to prepare and enjoyed with chutney or sambar.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately, grind into a smooth batter, mix with salt, and allow it to ferment overnight.
  2. Spread a ladle of batter on a hot pan, drizzle a little oil, and cook until golden and crisp.
